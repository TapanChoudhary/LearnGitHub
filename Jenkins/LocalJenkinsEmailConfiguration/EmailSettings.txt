1. Go to jenkins Home page
2. Click on Manage Jenkins
3. Go to Configure System
4. Move down to "Extended E-mail Notification" tab
5. Provide your mail "SMTP server" EXAMPLE : smtp.gmail.com
6. Click on advanced button
7. Check "Use SMTP Authentication" check-box
8. Provide your User Name 	Example : tapan********@gmail.com
9. Provide your Password 	Example : yourPasswordHere
10. Check the checkbox "Use SSL"
11. Provide smtp port 		Example : 465
12. Select content type from drop-down Example : HTML (text/html)

You can leave the rest, and modify them specific to job
